# SmartTime AI — Privacy Policy (Draft)

_Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD_

SmartTime AI helps schools generate and publish timetables. We process limited account and timetable data for this purpose.

## Data We Collect
- Account identifiers (email, uid, role)
- School timetable data (classes, teachers, subjects, rooms, constraints)
- Operational logs (job status, diagnostics)

## How We Use Data
- Authenticate users
- Generate timetable outputs
- Show role-based timetable views
- Diagnose generation conflicts and system issues

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with infrastructure providers required to run the service (e.g., Firebase/Google Cloud).

## Data Retention
We retain operational and timetable data for service continuity unless school administrators request deletion.

## Security
We use role-based access controls, access rules, and encrypted transport.

## Your Rights
School administrators may request:
- Access/export of their data
- Correction of incorrect data
- Deletion of data

## Contact
For privacy requests: support@smarttime.ai
